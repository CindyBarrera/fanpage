Short Summary

I'm way more excited than I should be. So Tate Langdon is a character in the first season of American Horror Story. He took a big part in the season actually. As most of us should know, he was the reason Vivien had an Antichrist child and why she died. He was also "a thing" with Violet. She's honestly my second favorite character. Anyway back to Tate, in the show he was a ghost that was trapped in the house where Violet lived. They became close and loved each other. In the end Violet finds out about what Tate did to her mother and she uses everybody's favorite line, "Go away Tate!". That is forever our favorite line from Violet.
