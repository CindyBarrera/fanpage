First we'll start with information on the show as a whole and the creators

American Horror Story(also known as AHS) is an American anthology horror television series that was created and produced by Ryan Murphy and Brad Falchuk. The show was released on October 11, 2011 and is still going on today. American horror story is known for falling into different categories: horror, anthology, supernatural, science-fiction, drama, and erotic thriller. (What is anthology? Good question! Anthology is a collection of poems or other pieces of writing) AHS can be watched on the channel FX every Wednesday at 10PM ET/PT time.There are currently a total of 5 seasons.
